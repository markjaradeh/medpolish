Welcome to Medical Polish!

Welcome! Witamy!

We would like to greet you here online and introduce our pioneering language elective: Medical Polish!

In 2017, an innovative group of Polish-speaking medical students at Loyola University Chicago Stritch School of Medicine came together to create the first peer-led Medical Polish language program for medical students and colleagues in healthcare. Our founders were the first of many 'Polish teachers' to follow at the medical school and revamp the elective's curriculum every year. Most importantly, by devoting their time to teaching Polish medical terminology, the Polish Stritch students have been addressing the language and culture barriers experienced by the prominent Polish-speaking immigrant population in healthcare and therefore improving health outcomes.

This year in 2021, our Medical Polish program is sharing its resources for the first time online for enhanced student learning and practice. Furthermore, we are extending the invitation to current and future healthcare professionals from other institutions to communicate with Polish patients in their native language and better understand the cultural determinants of our health and wellbeing.

Good luck! Powodzenia!
